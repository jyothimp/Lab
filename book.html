Featured Books

Pushkin : Swathantryabodhathinte Duranthagatha by O.N.V Kurup

A work containing ten critical essays analyzing the life and poetry of Alexander Pushkin, the pioneer of modern Russian literature and literary language, along with translations of twenty-three Pushkin poems. O.N.V.'s uniquely beautiful translation of Pushkin's exceptionally shining lyrics. This is the first study book on Pushkin in Malayalam.

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Paulo Coelho's novel that has captivated the whole world. A shepherd boy named Santiago travels from his native Spain to the Egyptian deserts in search of a treasure hidden under the pyramids. The Alchemist is the story of that journey filled with powerful simplicity and inspiring wisdom. Wonders awaited Santiago, who began his journey in search of that completely unknown treasure, worrying about whether he would be able to overcome obstacles. The truths and goodness of the world reveal to him the treasure that resides within him. Santiago's life story enables every reader to recognize the power of our dreams and listen to their own hearts. A work that turns the world of every reader upside down is born every decade - The Alchemist is one such work.

Explores the author's unique style where readers feel connected to the characters' struggles and triumphs, even within fantastical settings that echo reality.
